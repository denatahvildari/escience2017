\f0\fs22\fsmilli11040 \cf0 The profile of anthocyanidins and corresponding glycosides\
and phenolic compounds was determined using a HPLC system\
(Shimadzu, Korneuburg, Austria) consisting of a SPD-M10AVP photodiode\
array detector, a chromatogram integrator, a LC-10ADVP pump,\
and an online degasser. Data signals were processed on a personal\
computer (PC) running the LC solution Multi software (Shimadzu,\
Korneuburg, Austria). Analytical separation of anthocyanidins and\
phenolic acids was carried out using a Phenomenex Luna 250  4.6\
mm, 5 \uc0\u956 m (HPLC Services, Breitenfurt, Austria) column.}
